About SIAC

SIAC (Srujan Science & innovation Activity Center) inspires curiosity and learning through interactive exhibits, science shows, and educational programs.

Our Mission

We believe that hands-on learning builds creativity and critical thinking. Our exhibits encourage visitors to explore science in a fun and interactive way.

What We Offer

Science shows, experiments, workshops, and family-friendly activities that make learning fun and exciting.

Our Vision

To become a hub for science education where creativity meets innovation.
